08. Your Blockchain Community

Blockchain Community Resources

Our community is at the core of what makes Udacity so special. We have several platforms on which you can engage with your instructors, mentors, and fellow students.

Slack

Slack is our primary mode of communication, both during and after the Nanodegree program. There are channels dedicated to projects, cool campaigns, networking, and more.

We also host Office Hours in Slack every Tuesday morning, 11:00-12:00PM PST. Office Hours is your time to ask your instructors any questions you may have. If you are in a different time zone and unable to join live, feel free to post questions ahead of time so your question can still get answered.

For questions during or after office hours, please pin your questions to the channel to receive a response during office hours. Note: non-pinned questions may not be answered due to high demand. We will try our best to respond to questions as they come in.

Reviews

Throughout the program you’ll submit projects for review. In some cases, these projects will be reviewed automatically, giving you feedback as quickly as possible.

As the projects get more in depth, our team of reviewers will get involved to give you more personalized feedback. They will share what you did well, and coach you on the things you may need to work on.

Events

At Udacity, we work directly with experts at the top of their fields - and we’re inviting you to our conversations with them. Throughout your program, we will be hosting online and offline events with blockchain leaders who will share their insights into the field and the secrets of their success. Be on the lookout for invites to these exclusive events.

And that’s it! We’re really excited for you to get involved with our community.
