Meet the team!

At Udacity we are a team that cares deeply about the quality of your learning experience. We're so excited you've joined us and wanted to take a moment to introduce ourselves.

Curriculum Lead: Brandy Camacho

Hey, I’m Brandy!

For over 15 years I’ve focused my professional career in every corner of the tech industry.

Now, I’m leading blockchain initiatives here at Udacity. I’m looking forward to providing you with a fantastic program experience.

When not busy leading the blockchain initiatives, you can find me hacking software sandboxes, tinkering with my 3D printer or spending quality time with my beloved family.

At Udacity we’re a team, an organization that cares about you and your learning experience. Looking forward to our journey together.

Hi, I'm Jessica!

Here at Udacity, I’ve worked on the open ecosystem of Android, and have taught in the Android Developer and the Android Basics Nanodegree.
