15. Access to your Career Portal

The Career Portal: Your Path to Getting Hired

In your Career Portal, you'll find a personalized job search guide that includes 1:1 reviews of your resume, LinkedIn profile, and more.

How Do I Get To My Career Portal?

To access the Career Portal, look for the briefcase icon in your left-hand sidebar when you're logged into your Udacity classroom.

Unable to find your Career Portal?

As a beta tester, you do not yet have access to the Career portal.
