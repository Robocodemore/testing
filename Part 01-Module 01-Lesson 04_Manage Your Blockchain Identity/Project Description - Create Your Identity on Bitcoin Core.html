Submission Instructions

Evaluation

Your project will be evaluated by a Udacity reviewer according to the Managing Your Blockchain Identity Project Rubric. Be sure to review it thoroughly before you submit. All criteria must "meet specifications" in order to pass.

Submission

To successfully complete this project and meet the rubric criteria, provide a document (in either ".txt" or ".md" format) and just add the values for the following data:

  • Message:

Document your project answers in either ".txt" or ".md" format. You'll need to submit in a Zip file to be able to upload a ".txt" file.

What’s next?

Once your project is submitted it will be picked up by one of our project reviewers. They will provide detailed feedback based on your submission and get back to you within 24 hours. Once your feedback is complete, you will receive an email with details about your project review.

In the meantime, feel free to look through your next project or begin the next lesson!
