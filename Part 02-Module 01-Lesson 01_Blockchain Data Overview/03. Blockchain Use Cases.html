Do you need a Blockchain?

Below there are a few examples of applications that may or may not need a database. Think through some of these ideas to determine whether you think a blockchain would be helpful, and if so, which type?

We've marked our opinion as the correct answer, but it's possible to argue for a other options. The important part is to think through the examples, and be able to support why you may or may not want to use a blockchain.

Good luck!

Supply Chain Management

A shared supply chain management application to track product shipments across companies.
SOLUTION: Hybrid Blockchain

An anti money laundering application owned and maintained internally at a credit card company.
SOLUTION: Private Blockchain

Application that allows cities to share and verify government voting information.
SOLUTION: Public Blockchain

An application to track resource allocation across company projects.
SOLUTION: No Blockchain
